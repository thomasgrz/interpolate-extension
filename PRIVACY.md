# Privacy Policy

> What information do you collect?

we dont collect your information! there is currently not even analytics tracking in this extension.

it's meant to become an open sourced developer debugging extension -- this extension is yours and we wont be tracking your usage

> How do you use the information?

we wont!

all your extension configurations ("interpolation configs") are saved to your local browser storage.
we dont save those anywhere. nor do we track what you're doing with the extension.

if you have issues with this extension please open an issue!

> What information do you share?

none! what you do with this extension is your business. not ours.
